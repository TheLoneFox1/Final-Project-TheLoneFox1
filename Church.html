Trinity Episcopal Cathedral


Little Rock, AR


The mission of Trinity Episcopal Cathedral is to proclaim the Word of God by sharing our gifts, ourselves, and our properties through ministry to each other, our neighbors, and our community as we serve God and represent the Episcopal Diocese of Arkansas.

Image of the charity working

Trinity Episcopal Cathedral is a nonprofit organization. It is based in Little Rock, AR. It received its nonprofit status in 1939.y
